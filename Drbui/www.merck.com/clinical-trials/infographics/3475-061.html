A Phase III, Randomized, Open-label Clinical Trial of Pembrolizumab (MK-3475) versus Paclitaxel in Subjects with Advanced Gastric or Gastroesophageal Junction Adenocarcinoma who Progressed after First-Line Therapy with Platinum and Fluoropyrimidine (MK-3475 061)

At a Glance

This clinical research study will evaluate the investigational drug, pembrolizumab (MK-3475), as a treatment in advanced gastric or gastroesophageal junction adenocarcinoma. Pembrolizumab has been approved for use in some patients with melanoma, but it has not been approved for advanced gastric or gastroesophageal junction adenocarcinoma.

  • Condition: Gastric or Gastroesophageal Junction Adenocarcinoma
  • Study Treatment/Method (drugs): Pembrolizumab as a single treatment or standard treatment
    • Phase: III
    • Enrollment Goal: 720

Clinical Study Approach

WHAT WE'LL NEED FROM PARTICIPANTS:

A sample of your tissue and blood tests

Radiographic imaging about every 6 weeks

Study visits as required

  • Other requirements as decided by the study doctor.

The Study Drug

Pembrolizumab is an investigational drug for advanced gastric or gastroesophageal junction adencarcinoma. Pembrolizumab has been approved for use in some patients with melanoma, but it has not been approved for advanced gastric or gastroesophageal junction adenocarcinoma.

Participation & Eligibility

DURATION & REQUIREMENTS SUMMARY

You must have a diagnosis of advanced gastric or gastroesophageal junction adenocarcinoma. The study will last approximately 24 months.

 

Study Locations

We are planning on conducting this study in the United States and/or Canada.
