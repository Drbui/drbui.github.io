    Our work sparks and channels the creative energy that turns potential into fulfillment, moving toward tomorrow’s solutions in an endless cycle: from invention, to impact, to inspiration – and to the next invention.

    Whether their careers are just beginning to take shape, or they have achieved a leadership position in the organization, our employees are each inventing the future in their own unique ways.

     

    These are their stories:


    “ My assignment in the department of Infectious Diseases and Vaccines focused on exploring next generation sequencing capabilities for targeted viral genome sequencing. After analyzing gigabytes of data, I proudly presented our findings to the department. This internship greatly surpassed my expectations.”

    – Jessica McAnulty, University of Delaware intern, Merck Research Laboratories

    LEARN MORE ABOUT INTERNSHIPS AT MERCK

    “ Merck partners with an organization that gives students with disabilities who are in the STEM fields access to mentors, and it’s been really rewarding for me to be a part of that.”

    – Jay Schiller, director of Pharmacokinetics, Pharmacodynamics and Drug Metabolism

    LEARN MORE ABOUT DIVERSITY AND INCLUSION AT MERCK

    “ The military has taught me how to invest in people, how to get those people to work together and how to build strong teams. I’m really excited about helping bring different voices to the table to help those teams work better so we can make that next product come to market and get it to the patients that need it.”

    – Harriet Johnson, specialist, Merck Manufacturing Leadership Development Program

    FACES OF MERCK

    At Merck, we know that great ideas can originate from experienced team members as well as those who are just beginning their career journey. We value the fresh perspective and unique solutions these individuals bring to every area of our business.

    “ I’m a social media strategist. Social Media is still a relatively new space for pharmaceuticals and I feel like I’m really helping the company innovate and push forward.”

    — Jacqueline Rothman, senior marketing specialist, MerckEngage Upper Gwynedd, PA

    “ My group deals with process optimization to make sure we can get our product to as many people as possible.”

    — Jordan Green, associate specialist, engineer West Point, PA

    MY CAREER JOURNEY @ MERCK: FRANK CLYBURN

    Our employees meet daunting challenges head on in their quest to contribute to the health and well-being of people around the world. Perhaps there is no greater challenge than conquering cancer and that is what Frank Clyburn set out to do when he began leading Merck’s oncology team in 2013.

    “ The oncology business was truly a start-up. I started with a team that I could count on one hand. We put a cross-divisional leadership team in place and together we managed to bring one of our most innovative products to market.”

    — Frank Clyburn, president, Global Oncology Business Unit
