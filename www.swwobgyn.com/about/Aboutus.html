    Dr. Bui

    Dr. Bui received her medical training from the University Of Kentucky College Of Medicine in Lexington,Kentucky. She completed her residency in Obstetrics & Gynecology at the Texas Tech Health Sciences Center in Lubbock, Texas, serving as Administrative Chief Resident. Before starting her own practice, she practiced medicine at Plaza OB/GYN in the Houston Medical Center for several years.

    Born and raised in Vietnam, Dr. Bui moved to Lexington, Kentucky, with her family in 1999. Now a Texan by choice, she is excited to provide excellent care to the diverse population of women in our Houston community and build long-lasting relationships with her patients. Dr. Bui, her husband Alex, and their daughter Annabelle, are looking forward to becoming active members of the community. In her free time, Dr. Bui enjoys traveling, fine dining, working out, and dancing.
