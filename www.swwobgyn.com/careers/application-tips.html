    Things to Know Before Applying

    If you do not find a position that matches your interests at this time, that's okay. You can join our talent community to receive updates on career opportunities that are tailored just for you.

    Please Note:

    Merck does not contact candidates for employment via text message or interview candidates online using Google Hangout, AOL instant Messenger or any other chat service, for work-from-home positions.

    Merck does not mail, electronically send, or wire any payments to prospective job candidates for them to purchase equipment for employment with Merck.

    Merck never requires candidates to send money in exchange for, or as a condition of, an offer of employment with Merck.

    REASONABLE ACCOMMODATIONS AND THE ONLINE APPLICATION PROCESS

    Consistent with Merck's commitment to equal employment opportunity, Merck provides reasonable accommodations to qualified individuals with disabilities who need assistance in applying electronically for a position with Merck, unless doing so would impose an undue hardship. To request a reasonable accommodation for this purpose, please send an email to StaffingAADAR@merck.com. This email address is to be used solely to request an accommodation with respect to the online application process. Emails for any other reason will not be returned. Please note that all requests will be held in strict confidence and handled by Merck's Disability Management department. Under no circumstances will requests be disclosed to the person(s) responsible for making the hiring decision. Reasonable accommodation requests are evaluated on a case-by-case basis.

     

    THE PHYSICIAN PAYMENT SUNSHINE ACT (PPSA)

    The PPSA requires Merck to report payments made to reimburse interview-related expenses for candidates who are US-licensed physicians. Physicians covered by the PPSA include Doctors of Medicine, Osteopathy, Dentistry, Dental Surgery, Podiatry, Optometry and Chiropractic Medicine who are licensed to practice in one or more states in the U.S. This information will be reported to the Centers for Medicare and Medicaid Services (CMS) on the CMS online database website. For more information click here.
